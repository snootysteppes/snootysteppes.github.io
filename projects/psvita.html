FAQ

Can I have Minecraft: Enhanced and Minecraft: Modded at the same time?

+

Yes, you can install both versions of Minecraft simultaneously, as long as you follow the correct instructions for installation. Just install Minecraft: Enhanced USA or JPN Edition, and keep Minecraft: Modded to Europe edition (PSCB00560).
